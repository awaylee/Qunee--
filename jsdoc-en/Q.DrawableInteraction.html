Class: DrawableInteraction

Q.DrawableInteraction

new DrawableInteraction(graph)

During the interactive process, the prototype of drawable interactor class often needs to draw some temporary graphs or auxiliary lines. These graphs can be drew on the top canvas of Q.Graph#topCanvas and cleared at the end of the interaction. Customized interaction can inherit this class and rewrite Q.DrawableInteraction
