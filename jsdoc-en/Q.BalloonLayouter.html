Class: BalloonLayouter

Q.BalloonLayouter

new BalloonLayouter(graph, radius, gap, startAngle)

Bubble layouter Bubble layout is similar to tree layout, which is applicable for expression of tree graph. The layout is arranged in a round and is a high efficiency type of layout
